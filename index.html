— The Man & The Book —

Henery David Thoreau has inspired millions to look at life as more than a pursuit of the "stuff" we can collect and consume. His words and life taught us to seek knowledge, live minimally and to pursue true fullfillment.

Learn More About Thoreau

Fed up with collectivism and consumerism, Henery David Thoreau, borrowed a plot of land near Walden pond from friend, Ralph Waldo Emmerson. There he spent two years living minimally and pondering what would later become "Walden".
